About Me

I am a former student-athlete that graduated from Georgia Tech way back in 2009. I ran track and field, specializing in the 400 and 800m dash. I also ran on all of the relay teams. I was a unique runner for sure, as I could beat most the of sprinters over 40 meters but could aslo beat some of the cross country guys in the mile and even the 5,000. After school I was a personal trainer for a few years, running my own small business, before getting a job in supply chain analytics. Now, I'm looking to make my way into the world of web design and eventually become a software engineer!
